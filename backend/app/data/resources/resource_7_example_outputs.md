# Resource 7: Annotated Example Outputs
## What Excellent Analysis Looks Like vs. What to Avoid

**Purpose:** Set a concrete quality bar before real users see results. The AI should use these examples to calibrate tone, specificity, honesty, and structure. The difference between a good and a poor output is not length — it is specificity, honesty, and the sense that the user's actual answers were read.

---

**ANNOTATION: Why this output fails**

- No acknowledgement of the user's constraints, burnout, or real situation
- "Great fit" and "highly transferable" are generic — could apply to anyone
- No reference to any specific answer the user gave — feels automated, not personalised
- Salary ranges are given without any reference to the user's stated target — the gap is invisible
- No credential detail — "consider getting a CIPD" is useless without duration, cost, or access route
- No first steps — user has no idea what to do after reading this
- HR recommendation is based on "likes working with people" — a stereotype, not an analysis
- Q107 (dream role) is completely ignored
- Q108 (fear about daughter) is completely ignored
- The user's burnout score of 7 is completely ignored

## KEY PRINCIPLES DERIVED FROM THESE EXAMPLES

1. **Always open with situation acknowledgement** — especially for users with high burnout or financial constraints
2. **Every strength must be specific** — no generic "interpersonal skills" or "people person" language
3. **Every gap must have a resolution path** — credential + duration + cost + access route
4. **Salary gaps must be named** — not hidden in a range
5. **First steps must be actionable within 30 days** — no "explore your options" or "consider networking"
6. **Q107 and Q108 must always be addressed** — even if they complicate the recommendations
7. **Wellbeing signals must be acknowledged** — burnout score 7+ requires explicit recognition
8. **The user's own words should appear** — quoting or paraphrasing their open text answers signals genuine reading
9. **Never recommend something that violates Q078** — if they said they won't make a trade-off, respect it
10. **Dreams are not to be suppressed** — surface the path to them honestly, even if the timing is wrong
